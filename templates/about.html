About Our Mission

Empowering the digital world with truth and transparency

The Platform

The Fake Detection System (FDS) is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to identify manipulated media in real-time. By leveraging deep learning models trained on vast datasets of fake and authentic content, we provide users with actionable probability scores to assess the credibility of news, images, and videos.

Why it matters

In the age of generative AI and deepfakes, distinguishing truth from fabrication is harder than ever. We aim to restore trust in digital media through explainable AI.

How It Works

NLP Analysis

Detects sensationalism patterns and semantic inconsistency in text.

ELA Forensics

Analyzes Error Level Analysis and noise artifacts in images.

Frame Consistency

Checks temporal coherence and facial landmarks in video streams.
